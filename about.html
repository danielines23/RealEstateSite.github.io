About Me

As a professional in the Ithaca Real Estate Market since 2004, I have proven to be successful at providing excellent services for my buyers and sellers. My experience has equipped me with a nuanced understanding of local market dynamics and trends. I take pride in offering tailored, personalized service to meet the specific needs of each client. My goal is to make real estate transactions seamless and successful while contributing to the continued growth of the Ithaca community.

I have lived in Massachusetts, Maine, Colorado and Spain and there is no place that I would rather live than right here in Ithaca!
