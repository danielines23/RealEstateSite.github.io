Buying a Home in Ithaca

Buying a home in Ithaca? Things to do: looking at property, signing and accepting an offer, mortgage process, a week before closing, and the day of closing.

First Contact and Before Looking at any Property

  • New York State Agency Disclosure Form
  • New York State Housing and Anti-Discrimination Disclosure Form

Before Signing an Offer

  • Property Condition Disclosure Form (Required)
  • Agricultural District Disclosure (Optional)
  • Utility Surcharge Disclosure (Optional)
  • Lead Paint Disclosure (Optional)
  • Write a Deposit Check (Optional)

After Offer is Accepted

  • Deposit Check is Deposited into Escrow
  • Fax Accepted Offer to the Buyer's Attorneys
  • Get the Attorney's approval
  • Schedule the Structural Inspection and the Radon Test
  • Complete the Structural Inspection and the Radon Test
  • Renegotiate if Needed
  • Remove the Structural and Radon Contingencies
